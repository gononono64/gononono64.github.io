## `Cleanup(id)`
Clean up DUI and render target resources.

**Parameters:**
- `id` (`string`): DUI identifier.

**Returns:**  
*(None)*  

**Example:**
```lua
exports['mrctv']:Cleanup("example_DUI_id")
print("DUI cleaned up")
```
